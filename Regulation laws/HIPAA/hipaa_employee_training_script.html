1. What is HIPAA?

HIPAA is the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act that protects patient health information.

  • Privacy Rule: Protects PHI from unauthorized disclosure
  • Security Rule: Protects electronic PHI (ePHI)
  • Breach Notification Rule: Requires notification of breaches

Penalties: Up to $1.5 million per violation category per year!
