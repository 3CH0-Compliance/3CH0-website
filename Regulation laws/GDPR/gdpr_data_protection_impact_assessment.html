Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

Pursuant to Article 35 of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679

When is a DPIA Required? Article 35(1) requires a DPIA when processing is "likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of natural persons." This includes:
  • Systematic and extensive profiling with significant effects
  • Large-scale processing of special category data (Article 9) or criminal data (Article 10)
  • Systematic monitoring of publicly accessible areas on a large scale
  • Processing that uses new technologies
  • Processing that prevents data subjects from exercising rights
