3. DSAR Process Steps

1RECEIVE - Log request immediately. Clock starts Day 0.
2ACKNOWLEDGE - Send acknowledgment within 48 hours.
3VERIFY IDENTITY - Request ID verification within 5 days.
4ASSESS - Determine scope and exemptions by Day 7.
5GATHER DATA - Collect all relevant data by Day 14.
6REVIEW & REDACT - Remove third-party data by Day 21.
7RESPOND - Send response by Day 30.
8CLOSE - Update register and retain records.

4. Identity Verification

Before processing, verify identity using:

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Utility bill or bank statement
  • Security questions from account
  • Email from registered address
Third-Party Requests: Require written authorization or legal documentation (power of attorney).
