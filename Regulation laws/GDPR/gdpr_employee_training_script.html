8. Data Breach Response

‚è±Ô∏è 10 minutes

What is a Data Breach?

A data breach is ANY security incident affecting the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of personal data:

  • Unauthorized access to personal data
  • Loss or theft of devices containing personal data
  • Accidental disclosure (wrong email recipient)
  • Ransomware or malware attacks
  • Unauthorized changes to personal data
We have only 72 HOURS to report serious breaches to the supervisory authority. Every minute counts!

If You Suspect a Breach:

  1. DON'T PANIC - but act IMMEDIATELY
  2. STOP - Don't try to fix it yourself
  3. REPORT - Contact {{1.security_officer}} immediately
  4. DOCUMENT - Write down exactly what happened and when
  5. PRESERVE - Don't delete or modify any evidence
  6. WAIT - Follow instructions from the security team
