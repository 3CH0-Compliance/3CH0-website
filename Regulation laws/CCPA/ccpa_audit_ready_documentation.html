5. CONSUMER RIGHTS PROCEDURES

5.1 Right to Know (§ 1798.110)

  • Procedure documented
  • 45-day response timeline tracked
  • 12-month lookback period implemented
  • Extended lookback for data collected after 1/1/2022

5.2 Right to Delete (§ 1798.105)

  • Procedure documented
  • Service provider notification process
  • Third party notification process
  • Exceptions properly applied

5.3 Right to Correct (§ 1798.106)

  • Procedure documented
  • Commercially reasonable efforts standard

5.4 Right to Opt-Out (§ 1798.120)

  • "Do Not Sell or Share" link on homepage
  • Opt-out mechanism functional
  • Global Privacy Control honored (NEW)

5.5 Right to Limit Sensitive PI (§ 1798.121) - NEW

  • "Limit Use of Sensitive PI" link on homepage
  • Limitation mechanism functional
  • Service providers notified of limitations

5.6 Identity Verification (§ 1798.130(a)(7))

  • Verification procedure documented
  • Reasonable authentication standard
  • Verification data used only for verification

5.7 Authorized Agents (§ 1798.140(ak))

  • Agent verification procedure documented
  • Written permission or POA required

6. MINOR PROTECTIONS (§ 1798.120(c))

  • Under 13: Parental consent required for sale/share
  • Ages 13-15: Minor opt-in consent required
  • Age verification procedures documented
  • 12-month waiting period after denial respected
