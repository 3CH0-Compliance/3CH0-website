Article 1: Definitions

Capitalized terms not defined herein shall have the meanings in the CCPA (Civil Code § 1798.140).

"Business Purpose" — Use of Personal Information for Company's operational purposes as specified in Schedule A, or purposes permitted under CCPA § 1798.140(e).
"Consumer" — A natural person who is a California resident (CCPA § 1798.140(i)).
"Personal Information" — Information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked to a Consumer or household (CCPA § 1798.140(v)).
"Sell" — Selling, renting, disclosing, or transferring Personal Information for monetary or other valuable consideration (CCPA § 1798.140(ad)).
"Share" — Disclosing Personal Information for cross-context behavioral advertising (CCPA § 1798.140(ah)).
"Sensitive Personal Information" — Categories specified in CCPA § 1798.140(ae), including government IDs, financial account information, precise geolocation, and other protected categories.
