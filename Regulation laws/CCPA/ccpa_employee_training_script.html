1. INTRODUCTION TO CCPA

Trainer Script: "Welcome to our CCPA compliance training. Today we'll learn about the California Consumer Privacy Act and how it affects our daily work. The CCPA gives California consumers more control over their personal information and requires businesses like ours to be transparent about how we collect, use, and share personal data."

Key Points:

• CCPA applies to California residents' personal information

• Gives consumers new rights over their data

• Requires specific business practices and disclosures

• Applies to businesses meeting certain thresholds

2. KEY CCPA CONCEPTS

Trainer Script: "Let's understand some key terms under CCPA that you'll encounter in your work."

Personal Information: Any information that identifies, relates to, describes, or could be linked to a particular consumer or household.

Examples we handle:

• Customer names and contact information

• Purchase history and preferences

• Online identifiers and browsing history

• Professional or employment information

Business Purpose: The permitted uses of personal information for operational purposes.

3. CONSUMER RIGHTS UNDER CCPA

Trainer Script: "California consumers have specific rights we must honor. When a consumer contacts us with these requests, here's how we handle them."

Right to Know: Consumers can ask what personal information we collect, use, and share.

Our Response: Direct to privacy team, document the request

Right to Delete: Consumers can request deletion of their personal information.

Our Response: Verify identity, process through proper channels

Right to Opt-Out: Consumers can say no to the sale or sharing of their personal information.

Our Response: Honor immediately, update systems

Right to Correct: Consumers can request correction of inaccurate information.

Our Response: Verify and update records promptly

4. HANDLING CONSUMER REQUESTS

Trainer Script: "When you receive a consumer rights request, follow our established procedure."

Step 1: Document the request with date, time, and contact information

Step 2: Forward immediately to {{privacy_officer}} or privacy team

Step 3: Do not attempt to handle complex requests yourself

Step 4: Ensure we respond within 45 days

Step 5: Never discriminate against consumers for exercising their rights

5. DATA SECURITY PRACTICES

Trainer Script: "Protecting personal information is everyone's responsibility. Here are our security practices."

• Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication

• Lock computers when stepping away

• Only access personal information when necessary for your job

• Report any suspicious activity immediately

• Follow our data retention and disposal policies

KNOWLEDGE CHECK

Question 1: A California customer asks what information we have about them. What should you do?

Answer: Document the request and forward to the privacy team immediately.

Question 2: You notice a coworker looking at customer data they don't need for their job. What should you do?

Answer: Remind them about data access policies and report if behavior continues.

Question 3: A customer wants to opt-out of data sharing. How quickly must we comply?

Answer: Immediately - we must stop sharing their data as soon as we receive the request.
