3. CONTRACT REQUIREMENTS CHECKLIST

Verify that the written contract includes the following CCPA-required provisions:

3.1 Basic Contract Elements

  • Written contract is in place and signed by both parties
  • Contract specifies the categories of personal information to be processed
  • Contract specifies the business purposes for processing
  • Contract specifies the duration of processing

3.2 Prohibited Activities (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.140(ag), (j))

  • Prohibition on selling personal information
  • Prohibition on sharing personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Prohibition on using personal information for purposes other than specified business purposes
  • Prohibition on using personal information outside the direct business relationship
  • Prohibition on combining personal information with data from other sources (except as permitted)

3.3 CPRA New Requirements

  • Certification clause - Vendor certifies understanding of and compliance with restrictions (Required by § 1798.140(j)(1)(B))
  • Monitoring/audit provisions - Business may conduct assessments at least annually (Required by § 1798.140(j)(1)(C))
  • Sensitive PI limitations - Use limited to what is necessary for services requested
  • Right to Limit compliance - Must honor consumer requests to limit sensitive PI use

3.4 Consumer Rights Assistance

  • Obligation to assist with Right to Know requests
  • Obligation to assist with Right to Delete requests
  • Obligation to assist with Right to Correct requests
  • Obligation to comply with Opt-Out directions
  • Obligation to comply with Right to Limit directions
  • Response timeline specified (recommend 10 business days to assist Business)

3.5 Compliance and Notification

  • Requirement to notify Business if vendor can no longer meet CCPA obligations
  • Business's right to take remediation steps upon non-compliance
  • Sub-contractor notification and approval requirements
  • Sub-contractor contractual flow-down requirements

3.6 Security and Breach

  • Reasonable security measures requirement
  • Data breach notification requirement with timeline (recommend 48-72 hours)
  • Breach notification content requirements specified
  • Cooperation in breach investigation and remediation

3.7 Termination

  • Data return obligation upon termination
  • Data deletion obligation upon termination
  • Certification of deletion requirement
  • Retention exception only for legal requirements

4. SECURITY ASSESSMENT

  • Written information security policy in place
  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher)
  • Encryption at rest (AES-256 or equivalent)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee security training program
  • Incident response plan documented
  • Regular security assessments conducted
  • Penetration testing performed
  • SOC 2 Type II certification (or equivalent)
  • ISO 27001 certification
