🌐 GLOBAL PRIVACY CONTROL (GPC)

What it is: Browser/device signal that automatically communicates "do not sell or share"

Our obligation: We MUST honor GPC signals as valid opt-out requests

More info: globalprivacycontrol.org

❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Who is covered by CCPA?
A: California residents (consumers). Businesses meeting thresholds: $25M+ revenue, 100K+ consumers' data, or 50%+ revenue from selling data.

Q: Does CCPA apply to employees?
A: Yes, CCPA applies to employee data (exemption expired January 1, 2023).

Q: What's the difference between "sell" and "share"?
A: "Sell" = exchange for money/value. "Share" = disclose for cross-context behavioral advertising (even without payment).

Q: Do we need consent to collect data?
A: No, but you must disclose collection at or before the point of collection and honor opt-out/limit requests.

Q: What's the lookback period for Right to Know?
A: 12 months prior to the request. Can request beyond 12 months for data collected after 1/1/2022.
