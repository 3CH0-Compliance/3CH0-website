3. PAIA MANUAL (Mandatory)

IMPORTANT: The PAIA Manual is a mandatory document under the Promotion of Access to Information Act that every South African company must have. It details how anyone can request information from the organization.

PAIA Manual Status

Do we have a PAIA Manual? {{paia_manual}}

Status: REQUIRED

PAIA Manual Contents (Required)

  • Contact details of the Information Officer
  • Guide on how to use the Act (Section 10 guide)
  • Description of the organization's structure and functions
  • Categories of records available without a formal request
  • Description of subjects on which records are held
  • Request procedures and fees
  • Remedies available when access is refused
  • Description of services available to the public

PAIA Manual Maintenance

The PAIA Manual must be:

  • Updated regularly (at least annually)
  • Made available on the organization's website: {{website_url}}
  • Available for inspection at the organization's offices
  • Submitted to the Information Regulator upon request
