Processing that requires prior authorisation WITHOUT obtaining it is a criminal offence (fine or imprisonment up to 12 months). Do NOT commence processing until authorisation is obtained.

1. Categories Requiring Prior Authorisation

Category A: Unique Identifier Linking (Section 57(1)(a))

Processing unique identifiers (ID numbers, etc.) for purposes OTHER than originally intended AND linking with other responsible parties' data.

Category B: Criminal/Unlawful Conduct (Section 57(1)(b))

Processing criminal behaviour or unlawful conduct information on behalf of third parties.

Category C: Credit Reporting (Section 57(1)(c))

Processing information for credit reporting purposes.

Category D: Cross-Border Sensitive Data (Section 57(1)(d))

Transferring special personal information or children's information to countries without adequate protection.
