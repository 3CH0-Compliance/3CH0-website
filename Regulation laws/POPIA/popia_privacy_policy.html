POPIA COMPLIANT PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy complies with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact details (name, address, email, phone number)
  • Identity numbers and demographic information
  • Financial information for payment processing
  • Records of correspondence and interactions
  • Website usage data through cookies and analytics

3. Legal Basis for Processing: 8 Conditions for Lawful Processing

We process your personal information in accordance with POPIA's 8 Conditions for Lawful Processing:

  1. Accountability - We take responsibility for personal information under our control
  2. Processing Limitation - We process information lawfully and reasonably, collecting directly from you where possible
  3. Purpose Specification - We collect information for specific, explicitly defined and lawful purposes
  4. Further Processing Limitation - Further processing is compatible with the purpose of collection
  5. Information Quality - We ensure information is complete, accurate, not misleading and updated
  6. Openness - We document processing activities and notify you when collecting personal information
  7. Security Safeguards - We secure the integrity and confidentiality of personal information
  8. Data Subject Participation - You may access your personal information and request correction/deletion

4. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide products and services you request
  • To communicate with you about our services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To improve our products, services, and user experience
  • For internal record keeping and administrative purposes

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Legal and regulatory authorities when required by law
  • Third parties with your explicit consent

6. Cross-Border Transfers

If we transfer your personal information outside South Africa, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place as required by POPIA Section 72.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction as required by POPIA Chapter 3, Condition 7.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, in accordance with POPIA Section 14.

9. Direct Marketing

We will only send you direct marketing communications with your prior consent as required by POPIA Section 69. For electronic communications, we require explicit opt-in consent. You can opt-out at any time using the unsubscribe link in our communications or by contacting us directly.

10. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information (Section 23)
  • Correct or delete inaccurate information (Section 24)
  • Object to processing of your personal information (Section 11)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator
  • Institute civil proceedings regarding alleged interference with the protection of your personal information (Section 99)
