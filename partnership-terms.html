1. Premium Partnership Structure

This agreement establishes an exclusive legal partnership between 3CH0 Compliance Global ("Company") and selected legal professionals ("Legal Partner") with tiered compensation and equity participation.

2. Equity and Compensation Details

2.1 Global Company Equity

üèÜ 2% Equity Stake

Partners receive 2% equity in 3CH0 Compliance Global, vested over 4 years to reward long-term commitment.

Year Equity Vesting Cumulative Equity Conditions
Year 1 0.5% 0.5% Successful probation completion
Year 2 0.5% 1.0% Active partnership & performance
Year 3 0.5% 1.5% Continued growth contribution
Year 4 0.5% 2.0% Full partnership completion

2.2 Tiered Commission Structure

Base Commission: 15% Accelerator: 20%
Threshold: $10,000 Monthly Recurring Revenue
  • 15% Base Commission: Applied to all sales in your exclusive territory
  • 20% Accelerator Commission: Activates after reaching $10,000 MRR, applies to all revenue above threshold
  • 5% Whale Hunter Bonus: One-time cash bonus for landing clients worth $50,000+ annually
  • Monthly Payouts: Processed on the 15th of each month, minimum $100 payout

2.3 Global Exclusivity Rights

üåç Exclusive Territory Rights

Partners receive sole rights to represent and build the 3CH0 brand in their designated region. This includes exclusive lead generation, client relationships, and brand development activities.

3. Partner Responsibilities and Performance

3.1 Document Verification and Quality

  • Review and verify AI-generated compliance documents for legal accuracy
  • Ensure documents comply with regional regulations and jurisdictions
  • Maintain document templates with current legal requirements
  • Provide legal disclaimers and jurisdictional guidance

3.2 Client Advisory and Support

  • Respond to client legal queries within 48 business hours
  • Provide expert legal guidance on compliance matters
  • Escalate complex matters requiring formal legal representation
  • Maintain professional liability insurance ($1M minimum)

3.3 Business Development

  • Actively promote 3CH0 services within exclusive territory
  • Participate in regional marketing and partnership activities
  • Identify and pursue "Whale" opportunities ($50,000+ accounts)
  • Provide monthly performance and market insights reports

4. Territory and Exclusivity

4.1 Exclusive Rights

Partners receive exclusive rights to their designated territory, including:

  • Sole representation rights for 3CH0 compliance services
  • Exclusive lead generation and client acquisition
  • Protected territory from other 3CH0 partners
  • First rights to expansion opportunities within territory

4.2 Performance Requirements

To maintain exclusivity, partners must achieve:

  • Minimum $2,500 monthly recurring revenue by Month 6
  • Quarterly growth targets as mutually agreed
  • Active participation in partnership activities
  • Maintenance of professional credentials and insurance

5. Payment Terms and Conditions

5.1 Commission Calculations

  • Commissions calculated on net revenue (after payment processing fees)
  • Accelerator commission applies only to revenue above $10,000 MRR threshold
  • Whale Hunter bonus paid within 14 days of contract execution
  • Minimum payout threshold: $100 per payment cycle

5.2 Equity Terms

  • Equity granted as Common Stock in 3CH0 Compliance Global
  • Standard 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff
  • Subject to Company's Stock Option Plan
  • Acceleration clauses for acquisition or change of control events

6. Intellectual Property and Brand

6.1 Brand Usage

Partners may use 3CH0 branding materials in approved marketing activities within their exclusive territory.

6.2 Content Ownership

Legal content and document improvements become part of the 3CH0 global knowledge base.

7. Term and Performance Management

7.1 Partnership Term

Initial 2-year term, automatically renewing for successive 1-year terms upon meeting performance targets.

7.2 Performance Review

Quarterly performance reviews to ensure mutual success and address any concerns.
