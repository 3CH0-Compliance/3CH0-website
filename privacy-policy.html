Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we collect, protect, and use your data to provide exceptional compliance services.

1. What Data We Collect

We believe in transparency and only collect data necessary to provide you with exceptional compliance services:

  • Business Information: Company name, industry, and contact details
  • Contact Data: Email address and communication preferences
  • Compliance Requirements: Legal and regulatory information you provide for document generation
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data to improve our service experience

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is handled with care and used exclusively for:

  • Document Generation: Creating personalized compliance documents tailored to your business
  • Service Communication: Updates about your requests and important service notifications
  • Product Improvement: Anonymous analytics to enhance user experience
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting our regulatory obligations

3. Cookies and Tracking

We respect your browsing privacy and use technology responsibly:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality and service delivery
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve our platform
  • No Cross-Site Tracking: We don't track your activity across other websites
  • Data Ownership: We never sell your personal information to third parties

4. Your Rights and Control

You maintain complete control over your data with the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we store about you
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Document Access: Download your generated compliance documents anytime

5. Security and Protection

We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Infrastructure: Enterprise-level hosting with regular security audits
  • Limited Access: Strict internal controls and access limitations
  • Third-Party Vetting: All service providers undergo rigorous security assessments
